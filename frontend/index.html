🛰 What is AeroSentinel AI?

AeroSentinel AI is an operational deep learning system engineered for the automated detection and classification of aircraft in Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) imagery. Unlike optical cameras, SAR sensors can penetrate clouds, smoke, and darkness, making round-the-clock surveillance possible in all weather conditions — a capability critical for modern defense and intelligence operations.

Detections are overlaid with color-coded bounding boxes and compared against ground-truth annotations for transparent performance validation. A combined confusion matrix and live metrics update across every single and batch detection run in the session.

🎯 Real-World Applications

🛡

Defense & Border Security

Continuous wide-area airspace monitoring to detect unauthorized aircraft near restricted zones and national borders without operator fatigue.

🏛

Airport Management

Automated tarmac surveillance to track aircraft movements and parking bays even in dense fog or heavy rain when optical cameras fail.

🌊

Maritime & Coastal Patrol

Detection of low-flying aircraft and seaplanes over open ocean where cloud cover is persistent and optical coverage sparse.

🌍

Humanitarian & Disaster Response

Rapid assessment of airstrips in disaster-hit regions to coordinate relief aircraft logistics from satellite imagery alone.

🔬

Intelligence & Reconnaissance

Automated exploitation of satellite SAR data to monitor airfield activity and generate change-detection reports over time.

📡

Remote Sensing Research

A benchmark platform for testing new SAR detection architectures and domain-adaptation techniques in aerospace computer-vision research.
